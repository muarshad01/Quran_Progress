Many literary scholars describe this as a ring (chiastic) composition, where the opening themes (creation, choice, destiny) correspond to the closing themes (final judgment and divine will), while the middle highlights the revelation of the Qur'an and the call to steadfastness. The longest section (verses 5–22) vividly illustrates the qualities and rewards of the righteous, serving as the surah's main ethical example.
