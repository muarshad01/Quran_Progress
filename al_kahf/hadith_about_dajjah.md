* Hadith describe __Al-Masih ad-Dajjal (the False Messiah)__ as a major sign of the end times, 
appearing as a young man with one eye (blind in the left, looking like a floating grape), 
with the letters "K-F-R" (unbeliever) written between his eyes. 
He will appear between the Levant and Iraq, causing massive upheaval for 40 days, 
entering every city except Mecca and Medina. 

Key details regarding the Dajjal from authentic Hadith (principally Sahih Muslim and Bukhari):
* __The Ultimate Test__: The Prophet Muhammad (ﷺ) warned that the temptation (fitnah) of the Dajjal is the greatest catastrophe from the time of Adam to the Day of Resurrection.
* __False Miracles__: Dajjal will bring illusions of paradise and hell; his "paradise" is hell, and his "hell" is paradise. He will bring rain, revive the dead (a believer whom he cuts in two), and command the earth to grow vegetation, say scholars on Islamonweb English.
* __One-Eyed Appearance__: The Prophet emphasized that Dajjal is one-eyed, stating, "Your Lord is not one-eyed," Sunnah.com.
* __Protection Against Him__: Reciting the first ten verses of Surah Al-Kahf provides protection against him, Sunnah.com.
* __His End__: He will be killed by Jesus (Isa ibn Maryam) at the gate of Ludd (in Palestine).
* __His Followers__: Most of his followers will be from among the Jews and others, and he will cause many to doubt their faith due to his perceived miracles, according to Wikipedia and Faith in Allah.
